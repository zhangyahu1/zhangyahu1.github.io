About Me

I am currently a PhD candidate in Informatics Institute, Faculty of Science, University of Amsterdam, working in Computer Vision Lab under the supervision of Prof. Theo Gevers and dr. Shaodi You. My interests include human pose and shape estimation.

Previously, I received my master's degree and master's degree in School of Mechanical Science and Engineering, Huazhong University of Science and Technology under the supervision of Prof. Xinyu Shao and Prof. Ping Jiang in 2015.
